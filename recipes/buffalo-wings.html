Buffalo Wings

Description

A simple buffalo wings recipe.

Quick and easy buffalo wings made in the air fryer!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken wings
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Franks Red Hot
  • Garlic (minced)

Steps

  1. Dry chicken wings with paper towel and place in a large bowl.
  2. Mix in oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Place wings in air fryer basket in 1 layer (do not stack).
  4. Cook at 400ËšF for 8 minutes per side for a total of 16 minutes, or until wings are crisp and golden brown.
  5. Melt butter in a pan on medium-low heat.
  6. Add garlic and cook for about a minute. Add hot sauce and cook for another minute, mixing as it cooks.
  7. Remove from heat and toss the wings with the sauce.
  8. Serve with ranch and celery sticks. Enjoy!
